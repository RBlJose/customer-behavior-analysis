# 📊 Customer Behavior Analysis – End-to-End BI Project

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes customer purchasing behavior in a retail e-commerce environment to identify subscription gaps, loyalty patterns, and revenue optimization opportunities.

The analysis follows a complete data workflow, from raw data processing to executive-level dashboards.

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack
- **Python** – Data cleaning and preprocessing
- **SQL Server** – Data modeling and analytical queries
- **Power BI** – KPI creation and executive dashboard design

---

## 📊 Business Objectives
- Evaluate customer segmentation (New, Returning, Loyal)
- Analyze subscription behavior
- Identify discount impact on sales
- Detect revenue growth opportunities

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- 0% of female customers are subscribed → clear targeting gap
- Loyal customers show similar subscription rates as new customers
- Best-selling products maintain strong performance even without discounts
- Certain categories are highly discount-sensitive

---

## 🚀 Strategic Recommendations
- Redesign subscription model with segment-specific benefits
- Optimize discount allocation based on product elasticity
- Implement targeted campaigns for underrepresented segments
- Strengthen loyalty monetization strategies
